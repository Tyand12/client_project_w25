Tutoring

Need Academic Support?

Take advantage of tutoring resources to strengthen your understanding of course material.

Available Tutoring Services

Peer Tutoring @ UMSI

The peer tutoring program at the University of Michigan's School of Information (SI) connects students with knowledgeable peers who provide personalized assistance in various subjects. This collaborative program helps students clarify difficult concepts and develop effective study strategies. Tutors benefit by reinforcing their own understanding and gaining leadership skills, fostering a culture of mentorship and academic excellence at SI.

Sweetland Writing Center

The Sweetland Center for Writing supports students at every stage of the writing process. It offers consultations, workshops, and peer tutoring to help students brainstorm, organize drafts, and refine their final papers. Sweetland also assists multilingual writers and those working on long-term projects like theses and dissertations, contributing to the development of effective communicators at UM.

Math Lab & Science Learning Center

The Math Lab and Science Learning Center provide essential academic support for students in mathematics and the sciences. The Math Lab offers drop-in tutoring, while the Science Learning Center offers study groups and one-on-one sessions in subjects like biology, chemistry, and physics. Both centers foster collaborative learning and help students build problem-solving skills to excel in their courses.
