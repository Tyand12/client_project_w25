Professional Organizations at UMSI

REACH Consulting

REACH Consulting Group is a student-run, pro bono consulting organization at the University of Michigan that offers strategic advice and innovative solutions to local businesses, nonprofits, and startups. Comprised of talented undergraduate students from diverse academic backgrounds, REACH provides members with hands-on experience in consulting, equipping them with problem-solving and analytical skills. Members work in teams on real-world projects, tackling challenges related to marketing, operations, finance, and strategy.

Learn more here

Alpha Theta Delta

Alpha Theta Delta (ATD) is a professional fraternity focused on promoting excellence in the field of design. As a professional organization, ATD provides a platform for students interested in various design disciplines such as graphic design, industrial design, architecture, and more. The fraternity aims to foster a community where members can collaborate, share ideas, and develop their skills in a supportive environment.

Learn more here

Kappa Theta Pi

Kappa Theta Pi (KTP) is a professional co-ed technology fraternity that focuses on fostering a community of students passionate about technology and its applications. Founded to create a network of individuals with diverse technological interests, KTP provides members with opportunities for professional development, collaboration, and innovation. The fraternity supports its members through workshops, networking events, hackathons, and collaborative projects.
