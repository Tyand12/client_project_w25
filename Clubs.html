Professional Organizations at UMSI

REACH Consulting

REACH Consulting Group is a student-run, pro bono consulting organization at the University of Michigan that offers strategic advice and innovative solutions to local businesses, nonprofits, and startups. Comprised of talented undergraduate students from diverse academic backgrounds, REACH provides members with hands-on experience in consulting, equipping them with problem-solving and analytical skills. Members work in teams on real-world projects, tackling challenges related to marketing, operations, finance, and strategy. Guided by experienced mentors, the group delivers high-quality recommendations that help clients achieve their goals while also contributing positively to the community. This dual focus on professional development and impactful service makes REACH Consulting a valuable asset for students and clients alike.

Alpha Theta Delta

Alpha Theta Delta (ATD) is a professional fraternity focused on promoting excellence in the field of design. As a professional organization, ATD provides a platform for students interested in various design disciplines such as graphic design, industrial design, architecture, and more. The fraternity aims to foster a community where members can collaborate, share ideas, and develop their skills in a supportive environment. Through workshops, networking events, and collaborative projects, ATD helps its members gain valuable professional experience and connect with industry professionals. Additionally, the fraternity often emphasizes mentorship, career development, and the importance of interdisciplinary approaches in design. If you are interested in learning more about ATD at a specific university, it would be helpful to visit their official website or contact the chapter directly for the most accurate and updated information.

Kappa Theta Pi

Kappa Theta Pi (KTP) is a professional co-ed technology fraternity that focuses on fostering a community of students passionate about technology and its applications. Founded to create a network of individuals with diverse technological interests, KTP provides members with opportunities for professional development, collaboration, and innovation. The fraternity supports its members through workshops, networking events, hackathons, and collaborative projects, helping them to grow their skills and connect with industry professionals. By promoting an inclusive and dynamic environment, KTP encourages its members to explore the intersection of technology with other fields, stay updated on industry trends, and prepare for successful careers in tech-related roles. If you're interested in learning more about a specific chapter, such as one at the University of Michigan, it would be beneficial to visit their official website or contact the chapter directly for detailed and updated information.
